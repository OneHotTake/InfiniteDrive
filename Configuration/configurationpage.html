Content Filtering

InfiniteDrive syncs all content from your AIOStreams manifest. To filter content by genres or ratings, configure your AIOStreams manifest settings directly. InfiniteDrive applies parental controls based on MPAA/TV ratings fetched from TMDB.

Required for MPAA/TV ratings. Get free key at themoviedb.org → Settings → API.
